Education Success Story

New School Opens in Rural Kenya: 300 Children Now Have Access to Quality Education

December 15, 2024 ‚Ä¢ By Sarah Johnson

We're thrilled to announce the opening of our newest school in rural Kenya, made possible by the generous support of our donors and the hard work of our local partners. This milestone represents months of planning, construction, and community engagement.

The new facility will serve 300 children from surrounding villages, providing them with access to quality education for the first time. The school features six classrooms, a library, computer lab, and safe water facilities.

"This school will change the future of our children," said Mary Wanjiku, a local community leader. "We are so grateful to Hope Foundation and all the donors who made this possible."
